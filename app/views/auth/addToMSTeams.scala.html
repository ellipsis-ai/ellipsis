What is Ellipsis?

Ellipsis is an assistant that can perform tasks and provide information in response to your messages in chat.

Install useful skills, customize them, and create new workflows from scratch. Then speak to Ellipsis whenever you need.

Quick setup

1 Grant Ellipsis access to your Microsoft Teams organization
2 Add the Ellipsis App to one of your Teams
3 Type @@Ellipsis help in a channel
