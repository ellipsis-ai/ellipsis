Welcome to Ellipsis

The main way to use Ellipsis is through chat, but you can also modify Ellipsis here.

Behaviors

A behavior is something that your bot does or says when prompted with the right words. You can see what behaviors your bot knows, modify them, and program new ones.

Configuration

If you want to make any configuration data generally available for use in your behaviors, you can add or modify it here. This is a good way to provide re-usable credentials so that Ellipsis can integrate with other services to retrieve or modify data.
